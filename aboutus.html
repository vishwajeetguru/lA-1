About us

Mr. Akshay Shriwas (#Kumar Akshay) is a Rehabilitation Psychologist and founder of MindCafee group. MindCafee is a group of psychiatrists, psychologists, psychotherapists, and counselors united together to enlighten the power of the individual, by making this platform easy and knowledgeable. This platform help seekers understand human psychology and behavior by helping them to develop and improve Mental Health and rule out Mental Disorder through this awareness program. Mindcafee also discusses and guide people about different theories and therapies of psychology like Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Rational Emotive Behavioural Therapy (REBT), Psychoanalytical, Couple Therapy, Dream Interpretation, Mastering Conscious and Sub-Conscious Mind, Practical and Self Grooming approach for Self Introspection and many more. We want to make people aware of their Psychodynamics, to switch off autopilot mode, and take charge of life, by helping them cope up with Different scenarios and aspects of their life. We help them by teaching the right approach to make smooth going and gain control over the situation to lead a life in a better way.This can be achieved through Life Coaching, Transformation, and Personality Development.
